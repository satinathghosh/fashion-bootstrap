About Us

SILK Fashion established in 2022 at Kolkata with passion in women and kids wear has been a leading supplier of wide range of Sarees, Kids Pattupavadai, Kids Dhoti’s, Artificial Jewellery, South Bridal Jewellery, German Silver Jewellery, Handmade Jewellery and also extended to Language Vowels Wooden Letter Puzzle Board.

Over the years, we have developed and expanded our sales market both domestically and overseas, with export sales amounting to 50 % of our total annual sales turnover to countries like Australia, Malaysia, Singapore, Canada, Switzerland, Denmark, Sri Lanka, Germany, USA, UK, South Africa, Netherland and Mauritius.

We have tie up with saree weavers and jewellery manufacture from different parts of India, giving quality products with best competitive price in the market. The company’s management and staff are committed to total customer satisfaction by delivering the best products, services and support. This service excellence is further enhanced through a wide network of resellers.

  • We are constantly introducing new and trendy collections with affordable price to satisfy the growing demand of our customers
  • Together with our team of innovative and dedicated sales, we are well placed to meet customer expectations, especially in new product creations and market development.
  • We ensure our clients are completely satisfied with the quality of the products, services, and support they receive on an ongoing basis.
  • We strive to deliver products purchased from us are in excellent condition and at the fastest time as possible regardless of your location.
